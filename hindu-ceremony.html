The Hindu ceremony includes many different aspects which will include:
  • Jaimala (Garland Exchange)
    The bride and groom exchange flower garlands as a gesture of mutual acceptance
  • Havan and Vedic Rituals
    A sacred fire is lit, and the priest chants Vedic mantras, invoking blessings from deities
  • Saat Phere (Seven Sacred Rounds)
    The couple takes seven rounds around the sacred fire, each representing a vow to support each other in various aspects of life
  • Sindoor and Mangalsutra
    The groom applies sindoor (vermilion) on the bride's forehead and ties the mangalsutra (sacred necklace) around her neck, signifying marriage
